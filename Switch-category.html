PHONE;

The iPhone 15 comes in three storage sizes: 128GB, 256GB, and 512GB. These cost £799, £899, and £1,099, respectively.

The devices were announced on September 12, 2023, during the Apple Event at Apple Park in Cupertino, California, alongside the iPhone 15 Pro and 15 Pro Max. Pre-orders began on September 15, 2023, and the devices were available on September 22, 2023.

SD CARDS;

SD or microSD cards show up in Apple’s Files application like any other drive.

On the other hand, Lightning-enabled SD/microSD card readers use their own applications, not Files.

If you don't want to deal with third-party apps, use Apple’s Lightning to USB-A adapter with a standard USB card reader.
